# Sync Manual Changes

I have manually edited some files since your last change. Read the relevant files and understand what I changed before continuing.

## Steps

1. Re-read all files that are related to the feature/area we were just working on
2. Compare what you remember from your last edit vs what you see now
3. Identify:
   - What I added or modified
   - What I removed or restructured
   - Any patterns or conventions I seem to prefer
4. Summarize your findings before proceeding

## Output format

- **Files re-read:** (list)
- **Changes I noticed:** (bullet points)
- **Patterns I will follow going forward:** (bullet points)

Then ask: "Does this match what you intended? Should I continue?"
